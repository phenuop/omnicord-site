Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Omnicord ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our Discord bot and related services.

By using Omnicord, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree, please discontinue use.

Important: Omnicord is not affiliated with Discord Inc. or Cartoon Network. This policy covers only our data practices.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to operate Omnicord:

Data Type Purpose Retention Period
Discord User ID User identification, feature access Until account deletion
Username & Avatar Display purposes in bot interfaces Until account deletion
Server ID & Config Server-specific bot customization Until bot removal
Command Usage Analytics, abuse prevention 30 days

We do NOT collect:

  • Passwords or authentication tokens
  • Private messages not directed to the bot
  • Payment information (handled by Discord)
  • Sensitive personal information

Data Usage

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Provide and improve Omnicord services
  • Respond to user commands and requests
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics

No Selling of Data: We do not and will never sell your data to third parties.

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Secure server infrastructure

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to promptly addressing any vulnerabilities.

Data Sharing

We only share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With vendors who assist in operating Omnicord (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (with privacy protections)

We may share aggregated, anonymized data for analytical purposes.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your stored data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing

Note: If your account is suspended for violations or to protect the safety of our ecosystem and other users, we may retain necessary data related to the suspension for security purposes. All data will be completely removed when no longer needed for these legitimate purposes.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@omnicord.com.

Quick Removal: To immediately remove most of your data, simply remove Omnicord from your server.

Cookies & Tracking

Omnicord does not use cookies or tracking technologies on Discord. Our website may use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage statistics (opt-out available)

We do not use tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or cross-site tracking.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy as needed. Significant changes will be:

  • Posted on our Discord server
  • Announced via bot notification (if enabled)
  • Reflected with a new "Last Updated" date

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact Us

For privacy concerns or data requests:

Email: privacy@omnicord.com

Discord: Omnicord Support Server

We aim to respond within 7 business days.
